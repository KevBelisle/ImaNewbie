The Britannia Enquirer came about when I found I needed an added venue to take on the various issues that might not be suitable or timely for ImaNewbie. So I decided to introduce a new tabloid newspaper designed to keep my readers informed of the ongoing controversies plaguing our land and give me a chance to take a few shots at OSI/EA. The format is different than the regular toons with each cartoon limited to one frame, a headline, and if I am really feeling strongly about an issue, maybe even a full blown rant.
