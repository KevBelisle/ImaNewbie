Episode 94: Continuing with the Ultima "Renaissance" thing, this week ImaNewbie investigates the newly proposed Non Player-vs-Player zones that are supposed to be coming to Britannia in the near future.

This episode opens with ImaNewbie in Britain,
listening as the Town Crier announces the
establishment of the No-PvP zones.

Hardly believing his ears, ImaNewbie
rushes to see if it has truly come to pass.
Along the way he thinks to himself of the
glorious time that lies ahead.

Finally he reaches his destination.
But wait! What is this?

Poor ImaNewbie, even a Non-Pvp zone can't save his sorry ass. But never fear ... he'll be back next week, ever determined, ever cheerful, ready to take on his next adventure.
